Timing Guidelines
Recommended Values:
  • Pump: 60-120 seconds
  • Pre-wash: 300-600 seconds
  • Main Wash: 600-1200 seconds
  • Rinse: 300-600 seconds each
  • Pause: 5-30 seconds
  • Water Timeout: 60-180 seconds
Gerkon Settings:
  • Threshold: Depends on water level sensor placement
  • Debounce: 50-200 ms typical
